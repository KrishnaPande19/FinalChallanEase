Services

The PF Challan feature revolutionizes the tedious task of Provident Fund management for companies in India. With just 3-4 clicks, users can complete calculations and generate PF challans with 100% accuracy. By automating the process, this feature significantly reduces the time required from 45 minutes to mere minutes, ensuring precise calculations and compliance. Users can effortlessly input employee data, eliminating the need for manual entry and the risk of errors.

the ESI Challan feature simplifies the generation of Employee State Insurance (ESI) challans. By following a few simple clicks, users can generate ESI challans accurately and swiftly, eliminating the time-consuming manual process. This feature ensures compliance with ESI regulations while saving companies valuable time and resources. The seamless integration with the platform's automated processes enhances efficiency and accuracy in ESI management.

The Professional Tax Summary feature offers valuable insights into employees' tax data, aiding companies in tax analysis and compliance. By generating professional tax summaries, the platform enables companies to assess their employees' tax liabilities and obligations. This data-driven approach enhances financial planning and decision-making, empowering companies to optimize tax management strategies.

Under the Documents section, users gain access to essential forms and documents related to PF and ESI management. This includes Joint Declaration Form, Form 5 & 10, and Form 20 for PF, and Form 37 for ESI. By providing easy access to these documents, the platform enhances compliance and transparency in PF and ESI processes. Users can conveniently download and fill out these documents as needed, ensuring adherence to legal requirements and facilitating smooth operations.

The AI Chat Bot feature serves as a virtual assistant, providing prompt and accurate responses to user queries related to PF and ESI management. Users can access real-time assistance and guidance on various topics, including PF calculations, ESI regulations, and document requirements. The chat bot's intuitive interface and natural language processing capabilities ensure an effortless user experience, fostering better understanding and confidence in managing PF and ESI obligations.

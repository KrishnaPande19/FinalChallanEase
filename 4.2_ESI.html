  • Upload & make Payment


    To upload the ECR file and make payment on the EPFO Portal, follow these steps:
    1) Login: Visit the official ESIC website and log in using your credentials.
    2) Payment Section: Access Once logged in, locate and click on the "Pay e-Challan" option from the list of ESIC links or modules.
    3) Enter Employer Code: Enter your employer code and the CAPTCHA code, then click on "Search".
    4) Select Challan: After searching, a list of challans will appear. Select the appropriate challan number for payment.
    5) Choose Payment Method: You'll have the option to pay through SBI or other internet banking options. Choose your preferred method.
    6) Select Bank: From the drop-down menu, choose your bank. You'll then be redirected to the payment gateway of the selected bank.
    7) Enter Payment Credentials: Provide the necessary credentials to proceed with the payment.
    8) Review Challan Details: You'll see a page displaying complete details of the challan. Review the information carefully.
    9) Make Payment: Click on the "Pay" button at the bottom of the page to initiate the payment.
    10) Confirmation: Upon successful payment, you'll receive a payment successful receipt, indicating that your transaction has been completed.
